SUP YOGA

Pure bliss on the Ocean! Join us for an hour of SUP Yoga at Rip Curl School of Surf Sanur, everyday in Sanur's crystal clear shallow waters. It's new, it's fun and it's a great way to enjoy the Ocean and improve your fitness! Are you ready to have fun on the water, soak up some sun?

Group Price: Rp 450,000* per person (Minimum 3 person)

Private Price: Rp 600,000* per person (Maximum 2 person)

*Prices are subject to an additional 10% government tax


    Price Included
  • 1 hour SUP Yoga
  • SUP board hire
  • Bottled mineral water
  • Facilities: showers, changing rooms, lockers, and toilets
  • Transport and taxi boat out can be arranged with extra charge
  • All you need to do is bring yourself, a towel, swimwear, sunglasses and sunscreen.
